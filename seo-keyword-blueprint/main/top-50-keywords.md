# Top 50 Keywords

## Method

- `Est. US/mo` is a best-effort monthly U.S. estimate, not a Keyword Planner export.
- `Trend` is based on Google Trends or cluster-level inference when the exact long-tail had too little standalone trend data.
- `KD` is inferred as `Low`, `Medium`, or `High` from SERP competitiveness and public Semrush examples.
- `Priority` reflects business value first, then ranking opportunity.
