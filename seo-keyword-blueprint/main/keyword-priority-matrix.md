# Keyword Priority Matrix

## Scoring Model

Each keyword is scored from `1` to `5` on:

- `Relevance`: fit with the actual offer
- `Ranking`: realistic ability to compete in the next 6-12 months
- `Traffic`: likely search demand
- `Conversion`: likelihood to produce leads or assist sales
- `Effort`: ease of shipping the right page fast, where `5` means lower effort
- `Speed`: how quickly the keyword could affect pipeline if implemented well

`Total` is out of `30`.

- `Top priority`: `23-30`
- `Medium priority`: `18-22`
- `Long-term`: `17 or below`
