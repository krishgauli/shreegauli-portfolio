# Sitewide SEO Plan

## Objective

Turn the current site into a consultant-led SEO architecture that can rank for high-intent service terms, capture tool-driven traffic, and convert visitors with proof and internal linking rather than generic agency sprawl.

## Sitewide Page Map

- `URL`: `/`
- `Primary keyword`: `digital marketing consultant`
- `Secondary keywords`: `digital marketing services`, `seo consultant`, `google ads consultant`, `marketing automation consultant`, `dallas digital marketing consultant`
- `Intent`: Commercial
- `Purpose`: Establish the category, the operator, and the three highest-value service paths
- `Recommended H1`: `Digital Marketing Consultant for SEO, Ads, and Automation`
- `SEO title`: `Digital Marketing Consultant in Dallas | SEO, Ads, Automation`
- `Meta description`: `Work directly with Shree Krishna Gauli on SEO, Google Ads, and marketing automation systems built for measurable growth. Based in Dallas, working globally.`
- `Content sections`:
- Problem-aware hero with consultant framing
- Outcome-led services overview
- Proof strip with quantified results
- Featured case studies
- Why work directly with me
- Free SEO audit tool CTA
- Clear contact / booking CTA
- `Internal links`: services overview, each core service page, top case study, tool page, about, book
- `CTA direction`: Book a strategy call

### Sub-Case-Study / Proof Pages

Only create when supported by real material:

- `results-and-methodology`: aggregated proof page that explains how metrics are tracked
- `seo-audit-examples`: anonymized before/after audit examples
- `paid-media-account-rebuild`: breakdown page for campaign restructuring lessons
- `automation-playbooks`: selected workflow screenshots or process examples

These pages support conversion more than traffic. They should be built after the main case studies are expanded.

### Landing Pages

Only one landing page is clearly justified now:

- `free-seo-audit` or a stronger hybrid version of `/seo-tools`

Conditional landing page:

- `dallas-digital-marketing-consultant` only if more Dallas-specific reviews, proof, and internal links are added

Do not launch multiple city or niche landing pages yet.

## Internal Linking Requirements

- Homepage links to all four core services, the work hub, the writing hub, and the SEO tool
- Each service page links to one relevant case study, two relevant blog posts, the tool when relevant, and book/contact
- Each sub-service page links back to the parent service page and one proof page
- Each blog post links to one primary service page, one case study, and one adjacent article
- Each case study links to the matching service page, one related blog, and a CTA page

## Conversion Rules

- Commercial pages should end with a booking CTA, not a newsletter CTA
- Tool and blog pages should offer both a service CTA and a lower-friction audit / contact CTA
- Case studies should point readers to the relevant service, not a generic homepage CTA
