## Internal Linking Pattern

- Homepage links down into all pillar pages
- Pillar pages link to their supporting blogs and case studies
- Supporting blogs link back to the pillar and across to one proof asset
- Case studies link to the pillar and one supporting blog
- Proof pages link back to the pillar and the strongest matching case study

## Publishing Order

1. SEO pillar support
2. Paid media evaluation content
3. Automation proof content
4. Consultant-evaluation content
5. Local SEO expansion
