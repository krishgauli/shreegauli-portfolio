# Trend Analysis Summary

## Data Confidence

- No first-party Search Console export was available in the workspace.
- Trend direction was checked using Google Trends U.S. 5-year comparisons and one-keyword snapshots where available.
- Google Trends rate-limited some low-volume long-tail checks, so a few long-tail trend labels are inferred from the broader cluster rather than claimed as exact.
- Search demand examples were cross-checked with public Semrush keyword data and current marketing research.

## Main Trend Signals

| Cluster | Trend direction | Evidence | Strategic implication |
|---|---|---|---|
| `digital marketing consultant` | Rising | Google Trends checks showed recent growth versus the prior year; Semrush exposes meaningful consultant-level demand | Good homepage lead term |
| `digital marketing services` | Stable | Demand remains large, but the SERP is competitive and agency-heavy | Use on services hub, not as the only growth bet |
| `seo consultant` / `seo services` | Stable | Search demand remains strong and commercially important | Keep as core commercial pages even if competition is high |
| `technical seo audit` / `seo audit service` | Rising | Audit-related demand is strengthening and matches the site’s tool advantage | High-priority bridge from free tool to paid service |
| `free seo audit` | Stable | Reliable acquisition demand with lower-friction intent | Core tool page target |
| `seo audit tool` / `website seo checker` | Rising | Tool-intent queries are improving and the site has a real product | Best non-brand organic opportunity |
| `google ads consultant` / `google ads management services` | Rising | Recent trend checks and related Google Trends queries show healthy paid-search demand | Paid media page should lean harder into Google Ads wording |
| `social media consultant` | Stable | Social demand exists but is less differentiated and less defensible for this brand | Keep as a supporting offer, not the SEO center of gravity |
| `linkedin marketing strategy` | Rising | LinkedIn’s 2025 B2B measurement push reinforces stronger B2B platform focus | Use as the primary social long-tail, not Instagram-first content |
| `marketing automation consultant` / `marketing automation services` | Rising | Automation research and Trends checks both point upward | One of the best niche opportunities on the site |
| `n8n automation` | Rising | n8n-related interest and related queries are growing | Strong differentiator worth a dedicated sub-service page |
| Proof and comparison terms | Stable to rising | Lower volume, but stronger evaluator behavior | Excellent sales-assist content and internal linking targets |

## Implications For Priority

### Push Hard Now

- digital marketing consultant
- seo consultant
- free seo audit
- seo audit tool
- google ads consultant
- marketing automation consultant

### Build Carefully

- digital marketing services
- seo services
- social media consultant
- ppc management services

### Use As Supporting Terms

- consultant modifiers like `near me` and `Dallas`
- proof queries such as `seo case study`
- evaluation queries such as `how to choose digital marketing agency`
