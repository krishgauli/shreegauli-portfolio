# Gap Analysis

## Self-Check Summary

### Are the 50 keywords actually relevant?

Yes. The list stays anchored to the current offer: consultant-led growth, SEO, Google Ads, automation, and proof content. Social media is present, but intentionally not over-weighted.

### Is each keyword mapped to the right page type?

Mostly yes, with one important rule: several keywords are best treated as supporting terms, not standalone pages. That is deliberate and reduces cannibalization.

## Cannibalization Risks

| Risk | Why it matters | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Homepage vs services hub | Both could chase `digital marketing services` | Homepage owns `digital marketing consultant`; services hub owns `digital marketing services` |
| `/services/seo` vs `/services/seo-audit` | Both can drift into `technical seo audit` | Parent page owns `seo consultant`; sub-page owns the audit term |
| `/services/paid-media` vs `/services/google-ads-audit` | Both can target `google ads audit` | Keep audit page explicitly scoped and transactional |
| `/services/automation` vs `/services/n8n-automation` | Both can overlap on automation wording | Parent page owns the category; n8n page owns the niche keyword |
| Blog posts vs service pages | Evaluation posts may start sounding like commercial pages | Keep blog posts educational and route them into services with CTAs |

## Missing Page Opportunities

- `working-together` or `pricing` page
- `faq` page if objection-heavy sales calls continue
- stronger SEO audit landing experience around `/seo-tools`
- 3 fully expanded case studies
- 6 high-intent blog posts before broader content expansion

## Pages That Should Not Be Created Yet

- multiple city landing pages
- broad agency-comparison pages for every industry
- thin platform pages like separate pages for TikTok, X, YouTube, and Pinterest
- generic glossary content such as `what is seo`

## Weak Intent Alignment Areas

- Broad social media traffic can be noisy and low-intent
- Generic `digital marketing services` traffic will include buyers looking for larger agencies
- Some comparison terms educate well but may not convert immediately without strong CTAs

## Low-Quality Traffic Areas To Avoid

- broad beginner marketing education
- broad social media tips with creator or influencer intent
- generic software review content with affiliate-style SERP intent
- hyper-broad marketing definitions

## Internal Linking Gaps

- The SEO tool needs stronger links into SEO services and audit offers
- Service pages need more explicit case study links
- Blog posts need to be written to support service conversion, not exist as isolated insights
- The work hub needs stronger keyword framing and deeper page descriptions

## Final Recommendation

This strategy should be executed with depth, not scale. The site does not need fifty new pages. It needs:

1. A sharper homepage category
2. Three stronger commercial service pages
3. One tool-led acquisition page
4. Three deep proof pages
5. Six to eight genuinely useful blog posts
6. A disciplined internal linking system

That is enough to create a credible SEO system without turning the site into filler.
