# Sub-Case-Studies Blueprint

## Purpose

Sub-case-study pages are supporting proof assets. They should explain methodology, examples, or result patterns that help commercial pages convert.

## Recommended Supporting Proof Pages

| Page | Purpose | Build timing |
|---|---|---|
| Results and methodology | Explain how outcomes are measured and reported | Soon |
| SEO audit examples | Show anonymized before-and-after audit issues | Soon |
| Google Ads rebuild lessons | Show how campaigns were restructured | Later |
| Automation playbooks | Show sample workflows and process maps | Later |

## Content Outline Template

1. Problem pattern
2. What was checked or changed
3. What improved
4. What buyers should look for in their own situation
5. CTA to the matching service

## Internal Links To Add

- Matching case study
- Matching service page
- One supporting blog

## Trust Elements

- Real screenshots
- Process maps
- Anonymized metrics
- Clear methodology language

## Conversion Elements

- Soft CTA after methodology
- Hard CTA at end

## SEO Notes

- These pages are for proof reinforcement, not broad discovery
- Target proof-modifier terms and problem-aware phrases, not huge head terms

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Publishing proof pages with no real examples
- Letting them compete with the main case study pages
