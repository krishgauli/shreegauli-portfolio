# Homepage Blueprint

## Page Goal

Own the consultant-led category, explain the offer in plain language, and push qualified visitors into the right next step fast.

## Target Keyword

- `Primary`: `digital marketing consultant`
- `Secondary`: `digital marketing services`, `seo consultant`, `google ads consultant`, `marketing automation consultant`, `dallas digital marketing consultant`

## Search Intent

Commercial. Searchers want to understand who Shree is, what problems he solves, and whether he is worth contacting.

## Recommended SEO Setup

- `Slug`: `/`
- `SEO title`: `Digital Marketing Consultant in Dallas | SEO, Ads, Automation`
- `Meta description`: `Work directly with Shree Krishna Gauli on SEO, Google Ads, and marketing automation systems built for measurable growth. Based in Dallas, working globally.`
- `H1`: `Digital Marketing Consultant for SEO, Ads, and Automation`

## Heading Structure

- `H2`: Find the bottleneck before you buy more tactics
- `H2`: Services built around growth constraints
- `H2`: Recent results across SEO, paid media, and automation
- `H2`: Why work directly with me
- `H2`: Use the free SEO audit tool before we talk
- `H2`: Tell me what you're trying to grow

## Content Outline

1. Hero with one consultant-led message, one proof line, and two CTAs
2. Bottleneck chooser section: visibility, paid efficiency, content consistency, or manual ops
3. Four service blocks with outcome language and direct links
4. Results strip with hard metrics
5. Featured case studies with quantified outcomes
6. Short methodology section explaining audit, execution, measurement
7. SEO tool teaser for lower-friction entry
8. Final CTA with contact form and booking option

## Trust Elements To Include

- Quantified outcomes in the hero or results strip
- Client logos only if real and permissioned
- Direct operator positioning: no handoffs, no account managers
- Tool stack credibility: Search Console, GA4, Ahrefs, Google Ads, n8n
- Short testimonial close to the first CTA

## Conversion Elements To Include

- Primary CTA: `Book a strategy call`
- Secondary CTA: `Run a free SEO audit`
- One short contact form above the footer
- Service-selection prompt in the form

## SEO Notes

- Keep the homepage centered on the category term, not every long-tail
- Move the local modifier into supporting copy, title, and proof areas instead of forcing it into every heading
- Link the tool from the homepage body, not just navigation
- Use Person schema and BreadcrumbList

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Turning the homepage into a services dump
- Using vague agency copy instead of consultant language
- Hiding the strongest proof below the fold
- Sending all traffic to one generic CTA
