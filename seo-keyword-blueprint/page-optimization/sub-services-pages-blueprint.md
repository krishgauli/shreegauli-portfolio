# Sub-Services Pages Blueprint

## Purpose

Sub-service pages should exist only where they sharpen intent, shorten the sales cycle, or connect naturally to the current offer. They should not be created just to inflate page count.

## Recommended Sub-Service Pages

| URL | Primary keyword | Purpose | Build now? |
|---|---|---|---|
| `/services/seo-audit` | `technical seo audit` | Convert tool users and problem-aware buyers | Yes |
| `/services/local-seo` | `local seo services` | Capture SMB and local-intent SEO demand | Yes |
| `/services/keyword-research` | `keyword research services` | Offer a scoped strategy deliverable | Yes |
| `/services/google-ads-audit` | `google ads audit` | Lower-friction paid media entry offer | Yes |
| `/services/n8n-automation` | `n8n automation` | Lean into the clearest automation differentiator | Yes |
| `/services/crm-automation` | `crm automation consultant` | Tie automation to pipeline quality and response speed | Later |
| `/services/reporting-dashboards` | `reporting dashboard consultant` | Productize reporting and visibility work | Later |

## Shared Blueprint

- `Page goal`: win one narrow problem-aware keyword and convert into a scoped project or strategy call
- `Secondary keywords`: one parent-service term plus 2-3 close variants
- `Search intent`: commercial
- `H1 pattern`: `{Primary keyword} for {target buyer or outcome}`
- `Title pattern`: `{Primary keyword} | {benefit} | Shree Krishna Gauli`

## Content Outline Template

1. What the sub-service is
2. Symptoms that mean the visitor needs it
3. Audit or scoping process
4. Deliverables
5. Typical timeline
6. Before-and-after proof
7. FAQ
8. CTA to request the service

## Internal Links To Add

- Parent service page
- One related case study
- One supporting blog
- `/book`

## Trust Elements To Include

- Sample outputs
- Process screenshots if available
- Expected handoff and documentation process
- A direct statement of what is and is not included

## Conversion Elements To Include

- Form with project-type selector
- Short promise on response time
- Optional downloadable checklist only if it does not distract from the CTA

## SEO Notes

- These pages should be narrower and more transactional than the main service pages
- Keep them distinct from parent pages by leading with the specific problem and deliverable

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Building ten sub-service pages at once
- Repeating the parent service copy with only the keyword changed
- Targeting terms with no real delivery model behind them
