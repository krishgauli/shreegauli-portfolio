# About Page Blueprint

## Page Goal

Turn credibility into action. The about page should validate expertise, working style, and fit for founder-led or lean-team engagements.

## Target Keyword

- `Primary`: `Shree Krishna Gauli`
- `Secondary`: `digital marketing consultant`, `freelance digital marketing consultant`, `digital marketing consultant dallas`

## Search Intent

Navigational and commercial validation. Visitors already know the name or want to check credibility before booking.

## Recommended SEO Setup

- `Slug`: `/about`
- `SEO title`: `About Shree Krishna Gauli | Digital Marketing Consultant`
- `Meta description`: `Meet Shree Krishna Gauli, a consultant helping brands grow through SEO, Google Ads, automation, and clear performance systems.`
- `H1`: `About Shree Krishna Gauli`

## Content Outline

1. Short bio and positioning
2. Who Shree works best with
3. Experience timeline
4. Working philosophy and process
5. Tools and certifications
6. Industries and problem types
7. FAQ about working together
8. CTA to book or contact

## Heading Structure

- `H2`: What I actually help clients fix
- `H2`: Experience across SEO, ads, and operations
- `H2`: How I work on engagements
- `H2`: Tools, certifications, and systems
- `H2`: Who I am the best fit for
- `H2`: Questions clients usually ask before we start

## Internal Links To Add

- `/services/seo`
- `/services/paid-media`
- `/services/automation`
- `/work`
- `/book`
- `/contact`

## Trust Elements To Include

- Professional photo
- Specific years or role periods
- Named tools and certifications
- Real industries served
- One testimonial about communication or execution

## Conversion Elements To Include

- CTA after the methodology section
- CTA after FAQ
- Short line clarifying expected first response timeline

## SEO Notes

- This page should support entity SEO and conversion, not try to outrank service pages
- Add `sameAs` links to LinkedIn and GitHub in Person schema
- Mention Dallas once naturally and keep the rest global

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Repeating homepage copy almost verbatim
- Writing a long biography without buyer relevance
- Using this page to stuff service keywords
