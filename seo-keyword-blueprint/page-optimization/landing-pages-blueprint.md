# Landing Pages Blueprint

## Why This File Exists

Landing pages are only justified when they serve a distinct acquisition angle that the current core pages cannot cover cleanly. This site does not need a large landing-page program yet.

## Recommended Landing Pages

### 1. Free SEO Audit Landing Page

- `Recommended URL`: keep `/seo-tools` and make it a stronger hybrid landing page, or create `/free-seo-audit` and redirect or canonically support the tool
- `Primary keyword`: `free seo audit`
- `Secondary keywords`: `seo audit tool`, `website seo checker`, `seo audit service`
- `Intent`: commercial / informational
- `Page goal`: attract top-of-funnel searchers, let them use the tool, then turn qualified users into service leads

### 2. Dallas Consultant Landing Page

- `Recommended URL`: `/dallas-digital-marketing-consultant`
- `Primary keyword`: `dallas digital marketing consultant`
- `Secondary keywords`: `digital marketing consultant near me`, `seo consultant dallas`
- `Intent`: local commercial
- `Build only if`: more Dallas-specific proof, testimonials, and service-area credibility are added

## Free SEO Audit Landing Page Blueprint

- `H1`: `Run a Free SEO Audit and See What Needs Fixing`
- `Title`: `Free SEO Audit Tool | Check Titles, H1s, Speed, Schema`
- `Meta description`: `Audit any page for titles, meta descriptions, H1s, schema, canonical tags, and other SEO issues in minutes.`

### Recommended Sections

1. Tool hero
2. What the audit checks
3. What the results mean
4. When a free audit is enough and when a paid audit makes sense
5. CTA to request an SEO audit service

## Dallas Landing Page Blueprint

- `H1`: `Dallas Digital Marketing Consultant for SEO, Ads, and Automation`
- `Title`: `Dallas Digital Marketing Consultant | SEO, Google Ads, Automation`
- `Meta description`: `Work directly with a Dallas-based digital marketing consultant on SEO, Google Ads, and automation systems built for measurable growth.`

### Recommended Sections

1. Dallas positioning and who the page is for
2. Services offered locally
3. Local proof and credibility

5. FAQ
6. CTA

## SEO Notes

- One landing page for the tool is justified now
- The Dallas page is conditional, not urgent
- Avoid location-template expansion beyond Dallas until real local proof exists

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Building city pages with copied text
- Making the tool page only a form instead of a useful asset
- Splitting traffic between multiple nearly identical audit pages
