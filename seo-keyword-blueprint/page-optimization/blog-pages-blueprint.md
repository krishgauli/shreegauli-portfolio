# Blog Pages Blueprint

## Page Goal

Create long-tail content that supports service pages, answers buying questions, and feeds readers into case studies, the SEO tool, or a strategy call.

## Blog Hub

- `URL`: `/writing`
- `H1`: `SEO, Paid Media, and Automation Insights`
- `Primary role`: category hub, not a traffic page by itself

## Article Template

1. Direct answer in the intro
2. Why the problem matters to revenue or efficiency
3. Framework or checklist
4. Real examples or common failure patterns
5. When to do it in-house versus hire help
6. CTA to the matching service or tool

## H2 Template

- `H2`: The real problem behind the query
- `H2`: What good looks like
- `H2`: Step-by-step process
- `H2`: Mistakes to avoid
- `H2`: When to bring in outside help

## Internal Links To Add On Every Blog

- One matching service page
- One case study
- One adjacent blog
- One CTA page or tool page

## Trust Elements To Include

- Screenshots, examples, or actual process detail
- Brief references to real campaign or workflow lessons
- Honest notes on tradeoffs, not just best-case scenarios

## Conversion Elements To Include

- Inline CTA after the main framework
- Closing CTA matched to intent
- Tool CTA for SEO content

## SEO Notes

- One target keyword per article
- Use the exact question or phrase in the title and H1 where it reads naturally
- Add FAQ schema only when the article includes a real Q&A block

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Publishing generic educational posts with no business fit
- Writing listicles with no link path into services or proof
- Treating all blog traffic as equally valuable
